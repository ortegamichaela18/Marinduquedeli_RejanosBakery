Rejano's Arrowroot

There's something special in Rejano's Bakery's main product ingredient the arrowroot.
The name alone cannotes hope, as in shooting an arrow into the air, and stabilitiy, as in being rooted into the ground.

Business Owner

Carmelita the daugther of Crisostomo and Belen continued the tradition not only by bringing the product to Manila but also by sustaining the livelihood of arrowroot farmers in her province.

Business Store

Rejano's bakery has four (4) branches which is located in Boac, Torrijos and 2 branches in Sta Cruz Marinduque. Rejano's store is clean and elegant to look at, where you will not be hesitant to buy their products because you can assure that their delicasies are safe and clean to eat with.

The Best Product

The Arrowwoot cookies was their best seller product since it was their first product and recipe made by the mother of the owner as heritage. The delectable heart-shaped arrowroot cookies became popular even outside the boarders of Marinduque. Balikbayans and local tourists would always buy rejano's arrowroot cookies as take home gift or what Filipinos call "Pasalubong" to their loved ones not only in Manila but also abroad.

About Us

Rejano's bakery has been baking bread, cookies, and sweets over 70 years
It was formerly known as CASTILLO BAKERY then owned by Timoteo Castillo in Santa Cruz Marinduque.
It is made from first class high fabre starch extracted from Uraro (Arrowroot) tubers,
naturally grown in Marinduque. Purchasing this product will support the livelihood of Marinduque.
